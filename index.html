Dr. Mark E. Fuller, Ph.D.

Research Engineer

I am currently a member of the Physico-Chemical Fundamentals of Combustion research group at RWTH Aachen University. My current and past research interests include:

  • Experimental and theoretical examination of the role of nitrogenated compounds in combustion processes
  • Air-independent energy and propulsion including batteries, fuel cells, and monopropellants
  • Emissions measurement and modelling
  • Heat transfer

My broader researcher goals are:

  1. Development of sustainable and renewable energy solutions
  2. Harm reduction in energy generation both for human health and environmental effects
  3. Promote and contribute open-source and open-access data and resources
